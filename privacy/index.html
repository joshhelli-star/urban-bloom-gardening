This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use the Service and explains your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

Interpretation and Definitions

Interpretation

Definitions

  • Account: A unique account created for you to access our Service.
  • Affiliate: An entity that controls, is controlled by, or is under common control with a party.
  • Company: Refers to Urban Bloom Gardening. Also referred to as "the Company", "We", "Us" or "Our".
  • Cookies: Small files placed on your device by a website, storing browsing history and other information.
  • Country: United Kingdom
  • Device: Any device that can access the Service (e.g., computer, phone, tablet).
  • Personal Data: Information that identifies or can be used to identify an individual.
  • Service: Refers to the Website.
  • Service Provider: A natural or legal person who processes data on behalf of the Company.
  • Usage Data: Data collected automatically, generated by the use of the Service or infrastructure.
  • Website: Urban Bloom Gardening, accessible at urbanbloomgardening.co.uk
  • You: The individual using the Service or the entity on whose behalf they are using it.

Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

We may ask for personally identifiable information, including:

  • Email address
  • First and last name
  • Phone number
  • Address (including city, postcode, and region)
  • Usage Data

Usage Data

Automatically collected data may include:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited
  • Time and date of visit
  • Time spent on pages
  • Device identifiers
  • Mobile device data (e.g. operating system, browser)

Tracking Technologies and Cookies

We use tracking technologies like cookies, beacons, tags, and scripts to track activity and improve our Service. These include:

  • Cookies or Browser Cookies: You may set your browser to refuse cookies, but some functionality may be affected.
  • Web Beacons: Small electronic files to track usage, email opens, and gather site statistics.

Cookies are either:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain after you close your browser.

Types of cookies used include:

  • Necessary / Essential Cookies (Session): Ensure essential functionality.
  • Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies (Persistent): Remember cookie preferences.
  • Functionality Cookies (Persistent): Remember user preferences.
