Soil Improvement For Bigger Blooms

Unlock your garden’s true potential by improving the foundation everything grows in. Our organic soil improvement service transforms poor, depleted earth into nutrient rich growing medium that produces healthier plants, bigger blooms, and a naturally resilient garden that thrives with less maintenance.

What’s Included

Pricing: Can be included in garden maintenance session. Full garden quotes available based on area and soil condition. You are able to purchase mulch separately or with us.

Soil Analysis & Amendment

Test soil structure and pH, then improve with organic matter, composted manure, and natural minerals for optimal growing conditions.

Professional Mulching

Quality organic mulch applied correctly - suppressing weeds, retaining moisture, and slowly feeding plants as it decomposes.

Composting Systems

Set up efficient composting to recycle garden waste into nutrient-rich soil improver, creating a sustainable garden cycle.

Targeted Feeding Programs

Organic feeding schedules tailored to your plants’ needs, promoting vigorous growth and spectacular flowering without chemical dependency.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How much does soil improvement cost?

    ▾

    This can be included in the garden maintenance service. Prices do not include mulch or soil improver. You are able to purchase yourself and we can apply, or we can purchase for you also.

  • Can you improve heavy clay soil?

    ▾

    Absolutely. Organic matter, added drainage, and proper management transforms clay into beautiful soil over time.

  • How often does soil need improving?

    ▾

    Initial improvement transforms gardens, then annual mulching and composting maintains soil health naturally.

  • What’s wrong with chemical fertilisers?

    ▾

    They provide quick fixes but destroy soil biology, creating dependency and environmental damage.
